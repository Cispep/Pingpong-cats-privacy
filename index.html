Privacy Policy (English)

This Privacy Policy explains how the application “Pingpong cats” (the “App”) handles information about users.

1. Data Collection

This App:

  • does not collect, store, or share any personal data of its users,
  • does not contain third‑party advertising or analytics systems,
  • does not require the creation of a user account or login.

All data (for example, locally stored game scores) is kept only in the device’s local storage and is not transmitted to any server or shared with third parties.

APIs and SDKs of third parties:

This App is built using LibGDX, an open-source game development framework. LibGDX does not collect, track, or transmit any user data.

2. User rights (GDPR and COPPA)

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), users in the European Economic Area have the right to access their personal data, request correction, erasure (“right to be forgotten”), restriction of processing, data portability, and in some cases to object to processing.

In the United States, the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) protects the privacy of children under 13. The App is not designed to collect personal information from children and, in compliance with COPPA, does not require parental consent and does not perform any targeted tracking or profiling of children.

Because this App does not collect, store, or share any personal data (from either children or adults), the above rights are not practically relevant. However, if you have any questions regarding privacy (including under GDPR or COPPA), you may contact us at the email address provided above.

3. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version is always available at:
https://cispep.github.io/Pingpong-cats-privacy/
